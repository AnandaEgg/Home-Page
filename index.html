Our Mission

Our mission is to take one of the biggest gifts given to us in the past genration, Solar Panels, and provide access to underpriviledged people all around the world. From children in India who need electricity to study, to senior citizens in Nepal, there are countless groups worldwide who would immensely benefit from Solar Panels, and we are here to support them.
